How EchoEats Works

From discovering a deal to picking it up — here's everything you need to know about our dynamic pricing marketplace.

1
📍 Find deals near you
Open EchoEats and browse the live map or list view. All vendors within your chosen radius (default 5km) are shown with their current available items. Filter by dietary preferences — vegan, halal, gluten-free, and more — to only see what suits you.
2
📉 Watch prices fall in real time
Our dynamic pricing engine recalculates every 15 minutes. The closer a listing gets to its expiry time, the lower the price drops — automatically. You can see exactly how much time is left and how far the price could still fall before you decide to buy.
3
🛒 Reserve with a 15-minute hold
When you're ready, tap Reserve. The item is locked to you for 15 minutes — enough time to confirm payment and head to the vendor. No double-booking, no surprises. Pay securely via Stripe and receive a pickup confirmation with the vendor's address.
4
🌍 Track your impact & earn rewards
Every meal you save is logged. Track your CO₂ savings, unlock achievement badges (First Meal, 7-Day Streak, 50 Meals Saved), and compete on the community leaderboard. Streak bonuses give you early access to deals before other users see them.

The Science Behind the Savings

The Dynamic Pricing Engine

Every price on EchoEats is calculated using our core formula, balancing vendor revenue, consumer value, and food expiry pressure.

Price(t) = BasePrice × Decay(t) × DemandMultiplier

where t = time remaining until expiry

📐 Linear Decay
f(t) = t / T_total
Price drops at a steady, predictable rate. Great for consumers who want to know exactly when a good deal hits. Best for long-expiry items.
📉 Exponential Decay
f(t) = e^(λ(t−T))
Price holds steady then drops sharply in the final hour. Creates urgency and excitement near expiry. Best for high-demand items.
📊 Stepped Decay
f(t) = step([0.9, 0.7, 0.5, 0.3])
Price drops at clear milestones — easy for both vendors and consumers to understand and plan around. Most predictable option.
