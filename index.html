How EchoEats works

From discovery to pickup in under 3 taps. Prices automatically fall — the longer you wait, the bigger your deal.

1
📍
Find deals nearby
Search by location or browse the live map to see surplus food from vendors within your chosen radius. Filter by dietary preferences.
2
📉
Watch prices drop
Our dynamic pricing engine recalculates every 15 minutes. The closer to expiry, the lower the price — you decide when to strike.
3
🛒
Reserve & pay
Lock in your price with a 15-minute hold. No double-booking, no surprises. Pay securely and get a pickup confirmation.
4
🌍
Earn & track impact
Every meal saved earns you badges and CO₂ credits. Climb the community leaderboard and get streak bonuses.

Built for real impact

From AI-powered pricing to gamified sustainability — EchoEats is engineered to sell every last bite.

💰

Dynamic Pricing Engine

The heart of EchoEats. Our algorithm balances vendor revenue, consumer value, and expiry urgency in real time. Choose from linear, exponential, or stepped decay curves.

Core Formula
Price(t) =
BasePrice

📍

Live Map Search

Geospatial search powered by PostGIS. Vendor pins update in real time as you pan the map. Results within 1km load in under 50ms.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get alerted when your saved items drop to target prices, when a favourite vendor posts new stock, or 1 hour before expiry.

🏆

Gamified Sustainability

Earn achievement badges, track your CO₂ savings, and compete on community leaderboards. Streak bonuses reward consistent savers.

📊

Vendor Analytics

A live dashboard showing sales trends, peak waste times, pricing effectiveness, and revenue optimization suggestions.

🛡️

Secure Payments

Stripe-powered payments with instant vendor payouts. 15-minute reservation holds prevent double-booking with full concurrency protection.
